Can I use your music?

If you are making a remix, edit, montage, using my song as BGM in a video or in any other way transforming my work (THIS DOES NOT INCLUDE FNF MODS!) you don't need to ask me for permission, as long as you credit me it's all good :D


Can I put your song in my FNF mod?

If you would like to use one of my songs in your mod, I would ask that you contact me via Twitter so we can talk it over.
If you have made a remix of one of my songs and want to use that in your mod, then you don't need to ask, but I would like credit.
